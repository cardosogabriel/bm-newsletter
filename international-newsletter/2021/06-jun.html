Satsang, Narasimha Chaturdashi, 25 May 2021

 

Prahlad Maharaj had the firm belief that Bhagavan is omnipresent, that He is everywhere. There is not a single place that He is not.

 

You see, very often we want a God which is just in one place. No? Often we want a God which is designed how we want Him to be. In the same way you like to have your house the way you want or you like to dress up in a certain way, you also want a God that is seated in the temple and it is not anywhere else. Here Prahlad said, âNo, my God is not that which is seated in the temple. Because for my God the whole world is the temple. The whole universe is His temple. There is no single place which I can't call for Him.â


The determination and faith Prahlad is calling Bhagavan, can Bhagavan refuse that? He can't. He will manifest. There is no place that He is not. That's why to show that there is no place that He is not present, He came out from that pillar. He came out to protect his devotee.
