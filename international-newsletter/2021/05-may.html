KEEPING THE LOVE IN SEVA

Swamini Aparaajita Ma, Italy

Jai Gurudev Bhakti Marga Family,

 

During this special time in which we are living with our beloved Gurudev, so many gifts from His Grace are coming to all of us. One such gift is when we realise some difficulties in ourselves or our lives and we can change and transform them, growing in devotion and focus on God and guru.

 

Recently, several devotees have shared with me that they face difficulties while managing their daily devotee life. It seems that everyone has so many different and numerous things to do in the world - their job, family, daily life and so many Bhakti Marga activities and duties. They may lose the intimate joy and pleasure of doing seva or even having quality time with their devotional activities. In this way, one may lose themself and their focus.

 

As we know, the real quality of a servant - because we are servants, not only in the seva that we do in Bhakti Marga but in life because we are servants of Narayana-Krishna and servants of our guru – is to forget oneself. And instead, we often increase all our difficulties and problems and get heavy and complicate our lives even more. This is how we lose focus on the simplicity, love, taste, pleasure and fun of service. We become too focused on ourselves and not focused on Guruji and God.

 

Consequently, what we do becomes a duty, almost an obligation that takes away pleasure and enthusiasm of serving. Therefore, the service itself, even when it is born spontaneously and as a necessity of the heart may fail when we become overwhelmed because we are too full. 

 

Guruji reminds us that any technique, even the highest like Atma Kriya Yoga, or any seva we do, is worthless without love. The technique remains simply a technique and service remains simply a job. 

 

If it is not with this quality of love, we miss the real purpose of our sadhana. Life itself is a sadhana. Let’s hope that everyone can keep the focus more and more on His Lotus Feet so we can reach the point where everything we do becomes a sincere and joyful offering to Gurudev from our hearts.

 

Swamini Aparaajita Ma
