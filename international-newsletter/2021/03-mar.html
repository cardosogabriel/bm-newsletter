NEWSLETTER #71 · Mar 2021

LOVE ALWAYS WINS

Swami Kurunandha, SPN

Jai Gurudev

It is almost exactly one year ago that the first lockdown was imposed on the globe, and the Swamis and Rishis of BM wrote in a special public message to all the devotees that ‘we see this not only as a challenge but also as an opportunity.’

 

And what an opportunity it turned out to be!

 

In my assessment, I would say that Guruji and Bhakti Marga embraced the challenge in such a way, that the mission is stronger than ever before. Shifting a large part of our services to the online world has opened up many new fields of possibilities that weren’t in our awareness before. Guruji’s global family is growing fast every day and has used the special circumstances to learn and upskill itself. And more and more sanghas across the globe learn to connect in new ways.

 

Alone we are weak, together we are strong. Whilst seemingly the circumstances force us to be distant from one another, Guruji Himself has powerfully shown us that where there is a will there is a way. More people have had the opportunity to meet Him, and He is even more accessible and closer to more people than ever before. With the right mindset, we can’t help but see everything as a blessing in disguise.

 

Now that spring is arriving, the fear in the hearts of people that made them be frozen for a moment is melting, and the Divine Plan of global transformation is unfolding.

We were always asked to change. But we – I’m talking about humanity as a whole – was stubbornly refusing to do so, unless… unless the comfort zone that some of us enjoyed before gets deconstructed before our very eyes, including our own hypocrisy. That opens up a whole new dimension through which Love can enter into our lives and win over fear. When we lose something and then rediscover it, we can appreciate it again. I’ve heard Guruji share many stories of that nature over the last months.

 

I feel that our guru who has come not only for us but for all of humanity is offering powerful teachings to those who are open to it. Through it we can appreciate again that which counts: Life firstly, then humanity as a global family and last but not least, our most beautiful planet Earth, which has suffered much under the weight of our collective selfishness. Mother Nature has Her own way of asking for divine intervention. And we can be sure that in the end, like in the Puranic legends where good and evil are engaged in timeless battles, Love always wins.

 

With Love

Swami Kurunandha

PARAMAHAMSA VISHWANANDA

MESSAGE OF THE MONTH

Online Public Darshan 4 March 2021

 

Lord Shiva is the greatest devotee. He is an example of a great devotee who is completely surrendered to the Feet of Sriman Narayana. In the Shiva Purana, his wife asked him, ‘You are the Lord of the three words, on whom do you meditate?’ He said, ‘I meditate on the Feet of Lord Rama. I meditate on Sriman Narayana’s Feet.’ Lord Shiva is giving an example of where one’s mind has to be focused. You focus so much on worry, problems, fear and so on. That will not bring any changes in your life, in your world, nothing. But if you focus your mind upon the Lord’s Feet, then the transformation will happen. True love will awaken inside of your heart. That is what Shiva stands for. Shiva stands for that soul that is longing for their Beloved Lord. If you get in touch with Shiva inside of you, you will feel that longing.
