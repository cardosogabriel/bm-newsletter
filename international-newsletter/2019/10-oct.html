OCTOBER 2019, #54
Guruji’s visit to Riga, Latvia
Swami Vishwasharadaananda, Latvia
Jai Gurudev, dear BM family,
Our beloved Guru, Paramahamsa Sri Swami Vishwananda visited Riga, Latvia, on 09-12 September and inaugurated a new altar with new murtis of Sri Ram Darbar. He also gave a Darshan to over 1000 people at this time in a beautiful place in the very heart of the capital of the country. Looking back onto what just recently happened with all of us, we are speechless because it’s a lot for the mind to realize and understand how much Love and mercy we all experienced here. It’s a wonderful feeling to welcome Guruji to your home place and at the same time, enjoying Him being at home as well, like a big family living together with Him under one roof. He resides at every place where devotees love Him, serve Him, and worship Him every day. He can’t leave any place for a moment if we all stay connected to Him through practice of NavaBhakti (9 forms of Bhakti).
We feel that He is always with us here and it’s more than proved when He makes a visit. It’s evident for all of us that it’s only Him who makes all the new places to worship Guru and God; it’s Him who arranges everything to make it sufficient and effective. And by His Grace, we are all granted with these opportunities and possibilities to progress on our spiritual path, to become more and more true bhaktas of Guru and God, and to realize our true Self as eternal servant of Guru and God. To serve the Lord and His creation: this is what makes us all satisfied and happy.
We shared His Love and our inner joy of being with Him with over 1000 people and we were happy. We were happy because we didn’t try to possess Gurudev/God only for ourselves, and we didn’t try to hide Him only inside of us and enjoy Him alone. We were happy to share Him because the more we share Him with others, the more we feel Him, the more we understand Him, the more Love we feel and the more Loved we feel. What is also amazing about it is that it never stops, never has breaks or pauses. It’s ever-growing and ever-developing as long as we stay connected. Guruji visited us here only for few days but, if we really understand that He never leaves us, we will be always in His presence full of love, bliss and inner joy. Let’s pray to always stay connected.
Swami Sharada

SERVE A DEITY
We want to offer you an exclusive opportunity to serve the main deities at Shree Peetha Nilaya, the home of the Divine Mother.
What is the Serve A Deity Program?
This new opportunity enables individuals to actively assist the pujaris who serve the deities every day. Many people would like to assist in service towards the Divine but do not know how...now we are making it possible for you to help by sponsoring the deities.  
The donations provided through 'Serve A Deity' covers the costs for prasad, puja items, flowers, electricity, water, etc. Every time a puja is conducted in the temple, the blessing of this goes to every sponsor, no matter where they are, or from which country they come. In this way, you are helping the deity to be served and, at the same time, the blessing from the puja directly serves those who are supporting.

Message of the Month
Jai Gurudev,
You know, often people say, ‘Why do we need deities?’ The deity is an expression of the devotee’s love. If you say to somebody, ‘Express yourself to the void’, can you do that? You can, but people will think you a bit mad. But when there is a form, it becomes easy to express oneself. Take any relationship. When we talk about relationship, automatically there is a somebody, there is not an emptiness. You can’t have a relationship with air, saying, ‘I want to hug air.’
God is Almighty, He is everything, but due to the devotion of the people He chooses to limit Himself. How much love for us does He have that He can limit Himself even to a stone?
