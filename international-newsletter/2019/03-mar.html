MARCH 2019, #47
Longing for God
Swamini Dayamati, Japan
As a child I often had a longing: a longing for my home. But not where my parents lived, as I was in that home. The longing lay deep inside my heart. For a long time I didn’t really know what this longing meant for me.
As a child I had an English book at home, which I naturally then read in Japanese. It was the story of a young boy who was taken along by an old rich man (his grandfather maybe?), so that he could receive a life of care and better education. But since the life with the old man was not really liked by the boy, he left at some point to return to his home. There were, of course, many obstacles and difficulties along the way, until he felt near his parent’s house. And it is this part that I will never forget: The boy was again there, where he knew the old paths, trees, blossoms, and also the houses, and he was very happy! It was all described so nicely that I took it all into my heart and blessed it with lots of light and Love.
Today now I know exactly for what I had felt this longing, and for whom I had this longing.
After I met Guruji for the first time in 2008, I never again dreamed of any other Master, no one else appeared in my meditation, also not Mahavatar Babaji, but it was, in the beginning, all not very conscious.
Now I’m very often in Japan. I feel very good there and I’m happy to be there as well.
As long as my body plays along, I will keep returning to meet my beloved students and to work with them.
I now realise, that Guruji does not appear to me there; I can’t say never, but almost never. One morning though I knew all of the sudden, that He was with me the whole time, and that if He lives in my heart, how can He come to me? He is already here!
My big wish is to complete for Japan the translation of the Bhagavad Gita with Guruji‘s commentary.
Swamini Dayamati

Message of the Month
'VALENTINE'S DAY — A DAY OF CULMINATING CELEBRATION ON LOVE
Life, Beloved of God, God and me. To make it simple, it is God and me, two ends of the same Love. In between is Life and the story of Lover and Beloved.
What is this LOVE story about?
It is a story told by the Lover, about the Beloved, and also it is the story told by the Beloved about the Lover. And who is the Lover and who is the Beloved here? BOTH.
So the Lover of God wants to narrate the story of his Beloved. This soul wants to sing her song about her Beloved Lord's Love and the first lines are: When I see the night Sky, I can see the description of your heart, Beloved.
Why is the Sky dark?
So that the stars can shine.
