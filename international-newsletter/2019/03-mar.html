AUGUST 2018, #40
Trusting Gurudev
Swamini Vishwasarvatirthama, France
Jai Gurudev!!
A few days ago, all the Bhakti Marga family was dancing in the heart of Gurupurnima Festivities to celebrate and worship our Beloved Gurudev!! Physically in SPN and/or intimately in our hearts, no matter, we were all, as He likes to repeat it to us, 'in His Heart' just as 'He is in ours'.
How blessed we are, carried by His Grace and Love!!
He called us and found us, came to catch us from every corner of the planet, wherever we were, to remind us who we truly are, why we came here, and what is real life and its purpose: Just Love.
Our lives have changed since He called us and our priorities too. We are all well aware of this and we all know that we have to go further...
The question is, hearing all the glories of God and the lives of saints and great sages, how can we concretely, in our active lives, follow this path of Bhakti, of Love, of compassion towards all species?
Once, as I was facing world problems which took me some time, I asked Guruji: 'How can I fulfill my mission as a Swamini, having constraints that take me time to settle worldly affairs?'
Guruji answered me simply: 'Get to the point, I'll take care of the rest...!' Everything was said !!
What is the essence of the lives of spiritual people? Him, God, Love. Then the answer to the previous question is clear: organizing priorities in our lives and putting the essential first, trust Him, being sure that He will take care of us and is totally in charge. But we have also to do our part, to have this deep desire to change, and to follow His insights now, without waiting.
Not only does He give us life experiences to transcend what we have to change inside of us, but at the same time He is the model we have to follow. As He is serving us in each single spark of a second, He shows us, with love and patience, an inspiring path, the way to live through and with Love. Tirelessly, He repeats the same message: with the sincere will to change and a deep-rooted faith, knowing that it is by His Grace in response to our sincerity and longing for Love, living in bhakti, without expectation, we will attain the Lotus Feet of the Lord.
Let us love and give for the joy of giving, of offering. This deep joy inside of us is simply Him, the expression of His Love for us. It is Bhakti Marga itself, Guruji Himself!!
Prem Se Bolo Sri Swami Vishwananda Mahaprabhu ki...jai!
Much Love,
Swamini Vishwasarvatirthama

Kartik in Vrindavan with Paramahamsa Vishwananda
Join us for an unforgettable pilgrimage with Paramahamsa Vishwananda on the 6-17th November 2018 as we celebrate Kartik in none other than Krishna’s place of origin: Vrindavan. The month of Kartik is one of the most beautiful and most celebrated in Vrindavan. It is in this month that Krishna played most of His leelas: Damodara Leela, Rasa Leela, Govardhan Leela.
During the pilgrimage we will unfold and see all of them through the Grace of our Gurudev. We will celebrate Diwali and Govardhan Puja, and dance through the night of Kartik. Paramahamsa Vishwananda will lead every devotee on a pilgrimage inside their own spiritual heart as we visit ancient temples around Vrindavan like Banke Bihari, Radha Raman, Radha Damodara, Radha Shyam Sunadara and many more.
Check out the Pilgrimage information.
Silent retreat in Jagannath Puri, India
Experience a profound opportunity for deep silence, inner peace and communion with God in this Silent Retreat with Paramahamsa Sri Swami Vishwananda! The retreat will take place in Puri, which is one of the four Char Dham ('four abodes'), where it is believed you can get help to achieve Moksha (liberation).
The location we are visiting is considered highly sacred by Hindus and one must have a visit to 'Char Dham' during one’s lifetime. It is the first time ever that Guruji is taking a group of devotees to this sacred city.
This is a perfect place to practice meditation, Japa walks and sacred ceremonies such as Abhishekam, in the presence and under the shelter of the Satguru Paramahamsa Sri Swami Vishwananda. We will stay in an eco resort, which is a 20-minute drive away from the busy city of Puri, on the banks of the beautiful seashore of the Bay of Bengal. The resort is surrounded by the jungle, which gives this place a unique feeling of connection to Mother Nature.
Check out the Retreat information.
