APRIL 2019, #48
The Power of Choice
Swami Keshava, SPN
One cannot learn how to Love. It is impossible because you already are this pure Love. It is your essence. So the only thing you can do is serve the Guru in order to fully purify yourself from all fear, ego and negativity so that this pure Love can shine through. Do not go into the games of the mind, but take the conscious step of choosing to focus on the Guru’s Lotus Feet, for they are the only object that will give you peace of mind.
Continuously fix your mind on the form of Guru and you will see that, with patience, all obstacles will vanish; all fear will disappear. Because fear is the biggest obstacle on our path. Once, when Guruji was asked what was the difference between us and the saints, He simply replied, ‘The only difference is that the Saints do not worry!’ Which you can surely see and experience with Guruji ;) The Saints don’t worry because they don’t fear. They have full trust in the Lord and are fully surrendered. That’s the only difference. So trust in the Guru and surrender to Him for He will give you everything.
There is a very beautiful story in the Mahabharat. On a certain day, Krishna was having lunch. Rukmini, His wife, was serving Him. Krishna was One who had taken on the whole world, so He rarely got home. The chance of serving Him a meal did not come often, even for His wife. It was a privilege that she cherished and valued. Halfway through His meal, He suddenly got up and left without even washing His hands. She said, ‘Please, at least finish the meal before you go.’ He said, ‘No, one of my devotees is in trouble. I need to go.’ He went up to the gate, stood there for a moment, turned back and sat down to continue His meal. Rukmini asked, ‘What happened to your devotee?’ He said, ‘He was sitting in the forest, chanting my name, when a hungry tiger approached him.’ Krishna had seen that this man was totally immersed in the chant, calling Krishna with total involvement, and when the tiger approached, immediately Krishna had gotten up to go to him. He continued, ‘As I went towards the gate, the fool picked up a stone to protect himself, so I’ll let him do his business.’ This is the power of choice.
So always fully trust in the Lord – again, without fear or worries, for He is always by your side.
Svāśramaṁ ca svajātim ca svakīrti puṣṭi
vardhanam Etatsarvaṁ parityajya guror anyanna bhāvayet
Set aside your station and stage in life, caste, fame, wealth or means of attaining worldly success. Think of nothing but the Guru.
Sri Guru Gita, Verse 20

Message of the Month
'VALENTINE'S DAY — A DAY OF CULMINATING CELEBRATION ON LOVE
Life, Beloved of God, God and me. To make it simple, it is God and me, two ends of the same Love. In between is Life and the story of Lover and Beloved.
What is this LOVE story about?
It is a story told by the Lover, about the Beloved, and also it is the story told by the Beloved about the Lover. And who is the Lover and who is the Beloved here? BOTH.
So the Lover of God wants to narrate the story of his Beloved. This soul wants to sing her song about her Beloved Lord's Love and the first lines are:
When I see the night Sky, I can see the description of your heart, Beloved.
Why is the Sky dark?
So that the stars can shine.
