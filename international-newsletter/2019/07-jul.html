JULY 2019, #51
The Teachings of Our Guru
Swamini Vishwa Prabhaavati Mata, Spain
Many times, being with Guruji, I feel like watching a movie...He is, of course, the Superstar and the Director, and I’m just a mere observer in the background, taking notes and learning. Guruji teaches with every word, every look and every little thing He does. As He often says, He doesn’t forget not even for a second why He is here, so having a single-pointed mind concentrated on Him gives you the best teachings, for sure!
Although He was present with His body only the last part of the tour in Argentina and Peru, He was always fully present, ever since the very first moment He told me to go to the Latin American countries. One should understand how powerful is His word and His command when He tells us to do something. That’s really Grace, as we turn out to be just a mere instrument of Him, and He alone plans and does everything for us.
How amazing it is to see how His Love reaches so many people, when talking about Him or His teachings. But of course, when these people have the chance to experience His Love live, it’s one of the most inspiring teachings ever. This is truly what I have experienced all the time there, His Love everywhere, especially with His presence there, among so many people from different Spanish-speaking countries who were called by Him. But this doesn´t stop here, and that’s one of the most important tasks we have to do after people encounter Him in any way (whether it is in person, books, Facebook, Darshan, etc.): help them to realise that He is always present in their hearts, and that the best thing they can do is share that Love and create that community, where He is ever-present and helps us to step firmly in our path.
Jai Gurudev!
Swamini Prabhaavati

Mauritius PILGRIMAGE 2019
Join us for the Mauritius Pilgrimage, 19-29 October 2019, with Paramahamsa Vishwananda!
Mauritius is not only a sparkling island jewel in the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, but also the birthplace of Paramahamsa Vishwananda.
During our pilgrimage, we will visit the most sacred Hindu places in Mauritius, plus we'll learn about the childhood stories of Guruji in His family house. We will participate in three Darshans, plus Satsangs and daily temple services. To add to that, our hotel is surrounded by lush tropical greenery, just a few steps from a white sandy beach. Experience precious moments with Guruji in the beauty of Mauritius. 
Registration will be closed on 1 August 2019, so register now while space is still available!

Silence & Shakti: A Silent Retreat for Women in Vrindavan
Let silence reveal the Divine Mother within.  
Spend a week exploring the inner Shakti and uncover the feminine mysteries of our spiritual path with Swamini Vishwamohini under the guidance of Paramahamsa Vishwananda.
Experience the serenity and beauty of Braj Vasunder during this unique silent woman’s retreat at the foot of the majestic Govardhan. Located at a 5-star spa, practice meditation, take silent Japa Walks and enjoy the sacred ceremony of abhishekam in the mystic land where Lord Krishna enacted His Divine Leelas.
Don’t miss this opportunity to awaken your ‘inner Vrindavan’ and allow your True Self to blossom through the Grace of Paramahamsa Vishwananda.
Registration is open until 31 July 2019

Message of the Month
Jai Gurudev, everyone!
You see, incarnation, to take birth, is a mystery in itself, you know. It’s a deep mystery. Why it is a mystery? Because human birth is very special. You have heard many satsangs about it. You don’t get a human birth easily. But once you have attained a human life, how you handle that life is very important.
You see, everybody is called for something great in life, because you are not just the limited things that you think of yourself. You are more than that. Not only you…when I’m talking about ‘you’, I am talking about humanity itself. Very often people don’t realise why life happens, you know, why one comes on Earth, why one takes birth.
