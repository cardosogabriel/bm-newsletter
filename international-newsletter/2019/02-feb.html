FEBRUARY 2019, #46
To Attain the Lotus Feet of the Lord
Swami Vishwaaniruddhaananda, SPN
To be on a journey with our beloved Guruji and the spiritual family is an amazing grace. This pilgrimage to south India, which we just finished, is not just an outside journey but an internal one.
It is the inner journey of each seeker, with the Master and the fellow devotees. So many small goals lay on the way. Again and again we get a glimpse of God in different places at different times. Together with that, there are also some setbacks, illnesses to cure, obstacles to overcome, and inner demons to face.
That is what I experienced the last two weeks in my own experiences and by observing others. The Guru is the root and reason for each step we take. He makes this journey alive. Pleasure and pain, failure and gain. He presents them to us till we are healed from the vanity of this material existence; until we have our mind and whole being set solely on the Lotus Feet of God. That we fulfil the supreme purpose of every journey and pilgrimage, be it external or internal: TO ATTAIN THE LOTUS FEET OF THE LORD, TO LOVE AND TO SERVE HIM ETERNALLY.
The fact that God comes to this world into a human body to awaken the heart of each of us to this unconditional love, is the greatest proof for His love for us. Does He care for our pleasure or pain, happiness and sadness? I believe that actually, in the final stage, not anymore. Because all He cares about is that we transform. That we change: change the way we see ourselves, the way we see Him and the way we see our fellow brothers and sisters. Till one day, by His Grace we are able to see the same supreme reality of Shriman Narayana in all.
The one thing I have experienced again and again in this pilgrimage is that the love of the Master and the love of God is easily perceived through the love the fellow devotees have for Him. Once we can perceive the sincere, pure love they have for God and Guru, we perceive Him and His Love.
Therefore, I am the most grateful for the sangha Guruji has created, and for the love He has installed and awakened in each and every one of us.
SV Aniruddha

5 Elements Retreat
Join Paramahamsa Vishwananda for an inner and outer journey on Lanzarote, the ‘Island of Transformation’.
In this unique environment, you will explore the power of the five elements, both in yourself and out in nature.
This meditative retreat offers an opportunity to take a deep dive into the ancient knowledge of secret mantras and mudras that can help you better manage the effects of Earth, Water, Fire, Air and Ether in your own body.
Take advantage of this rare time away from your busy life to deepen your meditation practice under the extraordinary guidance of Paramahamsa Vishwananda. During this unforgettable week, you’ll enjoy hours of daily meditation out in the nature of this ruggedly beautiful volcanic island. You’ll also be treated to stunning views, vegan meals, hikes, and a swim in the ocean.
All explanations of the elements, mantras and mudras will be given only in English. Please note, translations are not available, so you must be able to speak and understand English to participate in this retreat.
Registration open until 21st of February, 2019
