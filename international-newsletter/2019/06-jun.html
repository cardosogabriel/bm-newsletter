JUNE 2019, #50
Lovetrons
Swami VishwaKanjalochanananda, Croatia
In my book that I am in the process of finishing, I wrote,
‘When you experience or meet Divine Love in your heart, even once in a lifetime, all other loves pale in comparison. But as It is evasive, palpable but still intangible by the five senses and the mind, It is very difficult to retain It. That's where the suffering of the most people begins.
‘In essence, we are made of that Love, each atom is bursting with that Love-essence. I like to call it “love-trons” (as opposed to neutrons, protons, biotrons, lifetrons, etc.). Lovetron is the most subtle yet life-giving substance. When we are in touch with our source of Divine Love, this source recharges our body-battery with these lovetrons. There is no device that can measure these units – only an open heart can reveal their presence to us. All we have to do is to keep the Divine Charger unobstructed for the limitless flow of lovetrons and let everyone be showered by its rays.’
In the forthcoming Just Love Festival, we are celebrating this Love – through music, song, food, friendship, yoga, Ayurveda, etc. Above all, we are celebrating it with Gurudev, who is the very source of that Love that we are searching for. As the iron needs a magnet to be recharged, so do we need our Guru to bring us ever closer to God. Recharged thus with these lovetrons, we are like little lighthouses and wherever we go, we will shine this Love to all.
Jai Gurudev!
In Divine Love,
Swami Kanjalochana

Mauritius PILGRIMAGE 2019
Join us for the Mauritius Pilgrimage, 19-29 October 2019, with Paramahamsa Vishwananda!
Mauritius is not only a sparkling island jewel in the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, but also the birthplace of Paramahamsa Vishwananda.
During our pilgrimage, we will visit the most sacred Hindu places in Mauritius, plus we'll learn about the childhood stories of Guruji in His family house. We will participate in three Darshans, plus Satsangs and daily temple services. To add to that, our hotel is surrounded by lush tropical greenery, just a few steps from a white sandy beach. Experience precious moments with Guruji in the beauty of Mauritius. 
Registration will be closed on 1 August 2019, so register now while space is still available!

Silence & Shakti: A Silent Retreat for Women in Vrindavan
Let silence reveal the Divine Mother within.  
Spend a week exploring the inner Shakti and uncover the feminine mysteries of our spiritual path with Swamini Vishwamohini under the guidance of Paramahamsa Vishwananda.
Experience the serenity and beauty of Braj Vasunder during this unique silent woman’s retreat at the foot of the majestic Govardhan. Located at a 5-star spa, practice meditation, take silent Japa Walks and enjoy the sacred ceremony of abhishekam in the mystic land where Lord Krishna enacted His Divine Leelas.
Don’t miss this opportunity to awaken your ‘inner Vrindavan’ and allow your True Self to blossom through the Grace of Paramahamsa Vishwananda.
