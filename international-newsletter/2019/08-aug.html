AUGUST 2019, #52
Just Love Festival 2019
Swamini VishwaParagatishwari, Mauritius
The Just Love Festival was held on 12, 13 and 14 July 2019.
When I reached the abode of Love, Patience and Unity – Shree Peetha Nilaya – I was amazed to see how many things had changed.
The decoration at the main entrance announced a big joyful event. Devotees were rushing up and down The Ashram, at times cleaning, painting, helping, and guiding in the building of structures and stalls for the big day.
Finally, the long-awaited moment arrived on Friday, 12 of July; the beginning of JLF 2019.
The decoration at the main entrance announced a big joyful event. Devotees were rushing up and down The Ashram, at times cleaning, painting, helping, and guiding in the building of structures and stalls for the big day.
Being in charge of the Mauritian stall, it was an immense pleasure to cook and serve food with the exotic blend of our island; full of love for the Divine. The best part of those days were when Gurudev visited our stand and the devotees sang and danced exclusively for Him.
The big podium was meant for concerts whereby artists expressed their devotional qualities with love and joy.
On the last day Gurudev ‘s melodious voice was finely tuned with Prakriti. The cool and soothing atmosphere of The Ashram blended with the musicality and hymns recited by Guruji, during which all the devotees danced with love, just love.
The day of 16 July was like the cherry on the cake which was JLF 2019. It added the blend of pure bhakti with the fragrance of the Guru Parampara. Devotees assembled in the garden around Gurudev, doing Pada Sevana Bhakti. Despite the chilling weather, devotees sat there gazing at Gurudev and listening to the experiences of devotees. It was a moment that will be cherished for a very long time.
Jai Gurudev!
Swamini VishwaParagatishwari

Maharashtra PILGRIMAGE 2019
Join Paramahamsa Vishwananda on a pilgrimage of a lifetime as He walks in the footsteps of the immortal saints and holy pilgrimage sites of Maharashtra, 10-20 January 2020. We will be visiting some of the most mystical and ancient temples that are shrouded in bhakti where the most auspicious saints have lived.
By the Grace of Paramahamsa Vishwananda, we will see Lord Vitthala at His abode in Pandarpur and visit the Samadhi of Shri Brahmachaitanya Gondavalekar Maharaj and Sadguru Shri Samarth Ramdas Swami.  
Also, for the first time, Paramahamsa Vishwananda will explore Kolhapur, where Lord Dattatreya is highly worshipped and, as the Avatar, took human form in this area.

Holi with Paramahamsa Vishwananda (Country Pilgrimage)
This year, devotees from Czech, Poland, Slovakia, Slovenia, Albania, Turkey, Croatia, and Romania are invited to join the most amazing and joyful festival of the year in Vrindavan with Paramahamsa Vishwananda. Holi is known as the festival of colours and the festival of sharing love. It is the most joyful time in India.
People light bonfires symbolising the victory of good over evil, forget about their concerns, and throw colours and water on each other in celebration of the fresh starts of spring.
If you are from Czech, Poland, Slovakia, Slovenia, Albania, Turkey, Croatia and Romania, join us for the Holi, 04-15 March 2020, with Paramahamsa Vishwananda!
Registration will be closed on 1 January, 2020
