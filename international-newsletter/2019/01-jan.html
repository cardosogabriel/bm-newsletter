JANUARY 2019, #45
Happy New Year!
Swamini VishwaPratibha, Canada
It’s a brand, spanking new year 2019. What are you going to do with it? Many will make a list and then beat themselves up for not following it, yet again. Some will laugh and not change...at all. No inner seeking, no wondering why the same things keep going wrong over, and over and...sigh...over again.
I remember being like that. I remember changing places, people, circumstances, hoping that this time I would hit the jackpot and my life would finally go smoothly. It never did. There were many bright moments, but the undercurrent of doom never went away. Finally, raked through the coals of a divorce, I began seeking.
It began with New Age books, but there was a nagging feeling they were only touching the surface of something deeper, and so I began digging deeper. A book about Mahavatar Babaji hit me on my head one day in an Encinitas bookstore, and I was introduced to the world of Paramahamsas. More studying, more reading, more sadhana.
I practiced surrendering. First in desperation when I was homeless with a baby, “God, if you get me out of this, I will do anything.” He got me out of it. A path opened up. Then I began to understand surrendering to the formless is not easy; God in a human body is needed to truly transform.
In life you will meet many teachers, many spiritual masters, many gurus. Only the Satguru can give you the realisation of God.
—Paramahamsa Sri Swami Vishwananda
After 30 years on the path, I realized I was looking for a Satguru. I was looking for Guruji.
He came to Canada in 2018. The second day in His presence in Vancouver, Canada, He took us to several temples. In one, sitting in silence, an overwhelming download of energy happened. That’s when I knew I had found the one I had been looking for. I could barely get up and follow Him out of the temple. But I knew I must never lose sight of THIS ONE.
It was obvious from things that were said that He knew everything that had happened. And He had always been with me. I was never alone through every test, every apparent abandonment. It all happened to shake the Maya off my eyes.
It was a test to see if I was worthy of Him. The Satguru takes responsibility for us for the rest of our lives. When He is giving so much, He needs to know we will be as loyal to Him as He is to us.
I have finally experienced real Love in the presence of the Satguru. Let this be your New Year’s resolution and gift to yourself.
Bhagavad Gita commentary by Paramahamsa Sri Swami Vishwananda:
Chapter 12, verse 5:
'Do you know how easy it is to make Him crazy? You just need to love Him. As you become crazy in love, He becomes even more mad in Love.'
Here’s to a loving 2019!
Jai Gurudev,
Swamini VishwaPratibha

Holi Pilgrimage 2019 with Paramahamsa Vishwananda, India
Join us for an unforgettable pilgrimage with Paramahamsa Vishwananda for Holi celebration 2019 in the place where Radha and Krishna are playing it Themselves, in Vrindavan!
Pilgrimage is open for the participants from all over the world!
For those of you who have participated in the Holi festivals with Gurudev before, there is no need to explain how amazing it is to play the colours with Lord Himself. But for those who have never been with Him in Vrindavan and especially for this amazing festival, we strongly recommend that you make the most of this rare opportunity!
In Vrindavan, Swami plays Holi with everyone for the whole week. We will participate in street kirtans and processions, take boat rides on the majestic Yamuna River and go for special darshans in the beautiful and ancient temples of Vrindavan. Guruji has called Vrindavan “The Heart of the Universe”.
During this pilgrimage, our group will be staying at the Bhakti Marga Giridhar Dham Ashram, at the Jiva Institute (Vedic Studies) and at other ashrams located next door. A group from each country is asked to travel together, so that we can arrange a group transfer from Delhi to Vrindavan for you.
Don’t miss this absolutely precious chance of spending an unforgettable time in India with Paramahamsa Vishwananda! We would love all of you to fully participate and enjoy this incredible 10 days of celebration and the most bright, joyful and astonishing festival of the year in Vrindavan!
Registration open until 31 January, 2019

5 Elements Retreat
Join Paramahamsa Vishwananda for an inner and outer journey on Lanzarote, the ‘Island of Transformation’. In this unique environment, you will explore the power of the five elements, both in yourself and out in nature.
This meditative retreat offers an opportunity to take a deep dive into the ancient knowledge of secret mantras and mudras that can help you better manage the effects of Earth, Water, Fire, Air and Ether in your own body.
Take advantage of this rare time away from your busy life to deepen your meditation practice under the extraordinary guidance of Paramahamsa Vishwananda. During this unforgettable week, you’ll enjoy hours of daily meditation out in the nature of this ruggedly beautiful volcanic island. You’ll also be treated to stunning views, vegan meals, hikes, and a swim in the ocean.
All explanations of the elements, mantras and mudras will be given only in English. Please note, translations are not available, so you must be able to speak and understand English to participate in this retreat.
Registration open until 02 March, 2019
