JUNE 2016 Edition, #15
Issued at Shree Peetha Nilaya, Springen/Germany
Jai Gurudev dear family,
These summer months are always the most exciting for Bhakti Marga. We have Guruji’s birthday, the Just Love Festival and Gurupurnima! It’s a time of great personal enjoyment and happiness but it’s also a very important time for another critical reason. It is a time we, as a family, welcome a lot of new people to our home. Fresh faces, fresh hopes and fresh potential for accepting Guruji into their lives and beginning a huge transformational process under the sheltering wings of Bhakti Marga. I want to remind you that we have a huge role to play in that as Guruji’s supporting cast. As his family, we are all jointly responsible for representing him and exemplifying who he is, and what he can offer to this world. We are called to be living examples of his teachings in action. As you all know, we are a Vaishnava movement.
We wear our tilaks, we sing Hari Naam and we exalt Lord Narayan in all his splendour. But that is just the outside. What is a Vaishnava really? What inner qualities should a Vaishnava have? First and foremost, a Vaishnava is humble. Sri Krishna says that the servants of His devotees are dearest to Him. Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu identified Himself as the servant of the servant of the servant of a pure devotee of Krishna. To serve the Lord directly is fantastic but very often leads to pride. After all, he is the Supreme, perfect and beautiful Lord. It is easy to submit to such a person, right? But how about serving someone imperfect? Someone limited? Someone who is ‘normal’? That requires true humility and true spiritual maturity.
Sri Swami Vishwananda teaches that we should give love and respect even if it is not reciprocated. That we should serve even without being served. Most of all, he says that to truly love God, is to forget oneself. Haribol! May Guruji bless us so that we may all answer that call and that we may begin to truly become Vaishnavas, not just on the outside, but on the inside too. This summer will be a wonderful opportunity to serve your brothers and sisters and to give plenty of love! I humbly ask you to please make your effort. After all, actions speak louder than words.
Jai Gurudev and all my love,
Swami Vishwarevatikaantananda, Shree Peetha Nilaya
BM Around the World
· Upcoming: Sri Swami Vishwananda’s birthday celebrations of Czech devotees in Prague.
· 'Cooking for Guruji' course in Helsinki, Finland: Mataji Lakshmi gave a cooking course called ‘Cooking for Guruji’ where she revealed various delicious dishes which Sri Swami Vishwananda himself likes.
· Austria tour with Swami Kurunandha: Satsangs, puja and yagna course, kirtans, Mantra Project, Jal Brahmacharini initiations and much more were organised in Salzburg, Graz and Vienna.
· Bhakti Marga Latvia on the Helsus Festival : Bhakti Marga Latvia presented itself during the Helsus Festival on May 20 and 21 where visitors could learn about Sri Swami Vishwananda’s mission of Bhakti Marga and the Bhajan Cafe in Riga.
·Swami Sharada visits Kiev, Ukraine: Many devotees were touched by the visit of Swami Sharada who conducted a Maha Laksmi Yagna, organized OM Chanting and gave various Atma Kriya Yoga lectures.
· Pre Event for the Swiss Darshan Tour in September 2016 with Sri Swami Vishwananda: A new regional group in Ticino organized a 3 day Bhakti Marga Festival - Rishi Pramod and Aaradhakananda came to join for a satsang, music workshop and a concert to promote Sri Swami Vishwananda’s Darshan tour this year.
· Weekend event with Swami Baladhara in Maribor, Slovenia: Devotees from Slovenia, Croatia, Czech Republic and Slovakia were blessed by participating in the Guru Puja, Satsang, singing of bhajans, yagna, OM Chanting and blessing with Maha Vishnu’s feet.
· Narasimha Jayanti celebrations with Swami Chakradhara in South Africa: Over 100 devotees participated in the most auspicious feast day of Narasimha where a Satsang, Kalash puja and abishekam took place.
· Narasimha Jayanti & BM group opening celebrations in Split, Croatia: More than 40 bhaktas were gathered to celebrate Narasimha Jayanti and the opening of the new BM group in Split where a Guru puja, abishekam to Narasima and a lecture of Swami Kanjalochana took place.
· Sri Yantra Drawing Course in Cudrefin, Switzerland: Devotees experienced a wonderful Sri Yantra Drawing Course in Cudrefin which was taught by devotees from Shree Peetha Nilaya.

Atma Kriya Yoga Teacher-Training
We are pleased to announce the dates for the next Atma Kriya Yoga Teacher Training! It will be from September 3rd to 16th, 2016. Send an email to teacher_training_2016@atmakriya.org if you feel drawn to be an Atma Kriya Yoga teacher and fulfill the following criteria: 1. Sri Swami Vishwananda is your Satguru. 2. Have practiced Atma Kriya Yoga every day for at least one year. In the email please include the following information: 1. Your legal name and, if you have, your spiritual name 2. The date you became a sadhak (student) of Atma Kriya Yoga 3. Country of residence 4. Language(s) you feel comfortable to learn in. Please note, the number of participants is limited.
Exclusive Vaishnava Pilgrimage – South India Pilgrimage with Mahamandaleshwar Sri Swami Vishwananda (5 December 2016- 18 December 2016)
Directly after the Inauguration of the Bhakti Marga Ashram Vrindavan, there is the opportunity to go on a pilgrimage with Guruji to Tamil Nadu, South India. Tamil Nadu has been the home of countless Acharyas, Saints and Avatars. It is also one of the main places of Shiva worship and various Maha Shakti Peethas. We will visit the mountain Arunachala, Maha Shakti Peethas, Sri Rangam – the main temple of all Vaishnavas and especially of the followers of Sri Sampradaya - and many more beautiful places and Saints.
Discover more at: www.bhaktimarga.org/southindia2016.
