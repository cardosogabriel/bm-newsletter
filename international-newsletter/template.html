AUGUST 2016, #17
'Teri Mahima Kahi na Jane
Teri Mahima Lekhi na Jaye'
The Glory of the Satguru can never truly be written or spoken about, but experienced in the heart. Such is its beauty. It is limitless and infinite. We all are truly blessed to be with Gurudev and to have experienced this beauty by His Grace.
As it is the month where we celebrate the appearance of Sri Krishna, it reminds me of the beautiful story that Gurudev told of Saint Kabirji in the Guru Gita. Kabirji grew up as a muslim but he was also considered to be an incarnation of Sri Krishna. It’s a reminder to us all that religion does not define your relationship with God. The relation of the Atma with Paramatma is only one of love.
In his heart he knew that Saint Ramanda was his Guru and through His deep devotion and perseverance he was accepted by his Guru. Kabirji had said, if he had to choose between Guru and God who would he bow to first, it would always be the Guru as through the Grace of Guru do we find God.
Every time I read his story it reminds me that every moment i can pray to be more devoted, sincere and to persevere. And to be Grateful for the most amazing treasure in all the universes, the Satguru.
Swamini VishwaMohini, India

Around the World
Upcoming 05-08 Aug: Weekend retreat with Swami VishwaAnashuyananda in Slovakia.
Upcoming 27/28 Aug: Events with Swami VishwaAnashuyananda in Prague, Czech Republic.
Slovak gathering with Swami Baladhara in Biely Kostol, Slovakia: Over 80 devotees from Czech and Slovakia gathered to meet with Swami Baladhara. The program of the day covered Guru Puja, blessings given by Swami Baladhara, singing of bhajans, Satsang and Maha OM Chanting.
Events with Swami Vijaya in South France: Many devotees were part of various events with Swami Vijaya, where puja, yagna, bhajan singing, Satsang and OM Chanting were organised.
Satsang with Swamini VishwaKishori Ma in Zurich, Switzerland: Swamini gave a Satsang about ‘Love as the key for good health and a joyful living’ where many new participants could join.
Swamis and residents of Shree Peetha Nilaya participated at the Devi Kanchi Kamakshi festival in Hamm, Germany: More than 30.000 Hindus attended a large Kanchi Kamakshi Devi procession where Swamis and residents of Shree Peetha Nilaya participated by doing seva.
Bhajan Cafe is present at the Holi festival in Riga, Latvia: Swami Sharada and devotees were happily selling sweets and cakes at the Holi festival and spread Paramahamsa Sri Swami Vishwananda's message of Just Love.
Atma Kriya Yoga keeps spreading in China: 7 new devotees were blessed by receiving Atma Kriya Yoga initiations in Guangzhou.
New Atma Kriya Yoga initiations in Spain: Swamini Prabhaavati Mata initiated six new enthusiastic Atma Kriya Yoga students in Barcelona, Spain.
Pre-event for Paramahamsa Sri Swami Vishwananda’s Darshan Tour in Malaysia: BM Malaysia is preparing Paramahamsa Sri Swami Vishwananda’s Darshan tour in October by organising free yoga classes, Babaji's Surya Namaskar and OM Chanting.

Atma Kriya Yoga Retreat In-Depth
We are happy to announce a new Atma Kriya Yoga Retreat In-Depth, that will take place in September at Shree Peetha Nilaya – Bhakti Marga Centre and Ashram, in Germany. This 5-day retreat offers you the unique opportunity to immerse yourself totally in your sadhana, away from the distractions of the world and daily duties. Through the practice of Atma Kriya Yoga twice a day, days of silence and contemplation, japa walk in nature, chanting of the Divine Name, guided meditations, you will explore, experience and love the power of Atma Kriya Yoga. “HOLI HAI!” Pilgrimage with Paramahamsa Vishwananda
From 9th – 19th March 2017, in Vrindavan, India, join the most joyful HOLI festival in the company of Paramahamsa Vishwananda!
HOLI: the festival of colours, is perhaps the most joyful time in India. People light up bonfires to symbolise the victory of good over evil, they forget all their concerns, and throw colours and water at each other, celebrating the coming of spring and new beginnings. Although Holi is traditionally celebrated for two days, in Vraj, the legendary land of Sri Sri Radha & Krishna where Vrindavan lies, it’s a vivid festival for 10 days! And it's slowly becoming a tradition that it is in Vrindavan where our beloved Guruji chooses to celebrate Holi.
This year for the first time, Guruji and the participants will be staying in the soon-to-be inaugurated Bhakti Marga Giridhar Dham Ashram. In the spirit of Holi, Guruji will also visit the holy spots of Vraj where the Leela of Lord Krishna continues. Do you want to be one of the lucky ones who will accompany him? Don’t miss this absolutely precious chance of spending these HOLI Days with Paramahamsa Vishwananda in Vrindavan!
For more information and registration, visit: bhaktimarga.org/holi-pilgrimage-2017
