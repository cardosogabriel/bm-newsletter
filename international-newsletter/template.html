MAY 2016 Edition, #14
Issued at Shree Peetha Nilaya, Springen/Germany
Jai Gurudev fellow devotees of our beloved Satguru Mahamandaleshwar Sri Swami Vishwananda,
Moving through the rest of the year we need to simply ask ourselves one question each day: How close of a relationship do we want to have with our Satguru? The answer you decide will determine your commitment and focus for serving your Guru. You see, Guruji in His infinite kindness has provided Bhakti Marga for us, which is the vehicle for Him to spread His message of Love around the world. However, it is also the vehicle that He uses for us to be able to serve Him, enabling us to have a relationship with Him. Everything we do on our spiritual journey, every service that we perform is simply to allow us to have a deeper relationship with our Satguru, for it is our Satguru Sri Swami Vishwananda that holds the key to unlock our hearts and free the love inside of us. It is this love that will bind Him to us for eternity, just as Damodara allowed Mother Yashoda to bind Him with Her ropes of love.
On July 15-17, the first Just Love Festival takes place at Shree Peetha Nilaya and it promises to be an occasion that will become a landmark festival, not only for devotees, but also for spiritual seekers all around the globe as we spread across Europe in the coming years. The vision of the festival is simple, reuniting in your relationship with the Divine and reconnecting your soul with God as we dance the night away in bliss. This year sees great new acts headlining at the festival which will set the standard for the years to come. It will be the talked about event of the year and “a must” to attend for any spiritual seeker. Directly after the Just Love Festival, we go straight into Guru Purnima on July 19th. Guruji especially moved the two together so that devotees could attend both and be with Him for 5 days of celebrations. If you have not made a plan to attend yet, I urge you to come and celebrate here with us. If you would like to know more, visit www.justlovefestival.org.
With Love,
Swami Vishwadhaanamjayananda, Shree Peetha Nilaya
BM Around the World
· Sanatana Dharma lecture with Swami Kanjalochanananda in Split: Swami Kanjalochanananda gave a lecture on Sanatana Dharma where he, through real-life situations, showed the importance of incarnating on Earth and why it’s important to perform one’s worldly duties.
· Czech Pilgrimage to Shree Peetha Nilaya with Swami Baladhara: Over 280 devotees were blessed by participating in Darshan and satsang with Sri Swami Vishwananda. Included were lectures, seva, children’s program, meditation in the Babaji cave, art tour, prayers and much more.
· Events with Swami Chakradhara in South Africa: In South Africa, a Maha OM Chanting took place with more than 90 devotees. Also in April, there were Ram Navami celebrations with a Kalash puja and yagna at the Maha Shanta Narasimha temple and an Atma Kriya Yoga course.
· Satsang & puja tour with Swami Madhava in South Germany: Story telling about Sri Swami Vishwananda, satsangs, puja and other activities were organised as Swami Madhava traveled around Germany.
· Polish Pilgrimage to Shree Peetha Nilaya: Over 150 devotees from Poland experienced a joyful time by participating in the Ram Navami celebrations at Shree Peetha Nilaya. Not only that, but there was satsang and Darshan given by Sri Swami Vishwananda. The group also participated in a guided tour through the ashram and attended a BM Academy class about Lord Rama and the epic Ramayana.
· Event weekend with Swami Anashuya in Slovenia: Swami Anashuya gave a satsang about the Guru Gita, blessed devotees with Sri Swami Vishwananda’s padukas and conducted a yagna.
· Satchitananda Vigraha Ramachandra Temple in Riga, Latvia: Swami Revatikaanta gave a class on the Guru Gita addressing the relationship between Guru and devotees as well how to serve and help the mission.
· 5-City tour with Swamini Godaavari in Romania: New BM groups were installed, pujas, satsangs, new Atma Kriya Yoga & Simply Meditation initiations and plenty more took place.
· Bhakti Marga Italy attended a Yoga Meeting Festival in Merano, Italy:OM Chanting, satsang of Swami Pranavananda, presentation about Sri Swami Vishwananda & Bhakti Marga, bhajan singing and other activities took place.
· “Firsts” for Bhakti Marga: a. A 24 hours OM Chanting with more than 250 participants and satsang with Swami Sharada took place in Vilnius, Lithuania;
b. First OM Chanting in Jilin, China, where around 40 participants attended;
c. OM Chanting also spreads in Latin America: For the first time various OM Chanting sessions were organised in Arequipa & Lima, Peru and Medellín, Colombia.
d. First OM Chanting in Kathmandu near the Pashupatinath area of Nepal.
e. First OM Chanting in the Fayaoum Oasis, Egypt

Atma Kriya Yoga Teacher-Training
We are pleased to announce the dates for the next Atma Kriya Yoga Teacher Training! It will be from September 3rd to 16th, 2016. Send an email to teacher_training_2016@atmakriya.org if you feel drawn to be an Atma Kriya Yoga teacher and fulfill the following criteria: 1. Sri Swami Vishwananda is your Satguru. 2. Have practiced Atma Kriya Yoga every day for at least one year. In the email please include the following information: 1. Your legal name and, if you have, your spiritual name 2. The date you became a sadhak (student) of Atma Kriya Yoga 3. Country of residence 4. Language(s) you feel comfortable to learn in. Please note, the number of participants is limited.
Sacred Places of Tamil Nadu – South India Pilgrimage with Mahamandaleshwar Sri Swami Vishwananda (5 December 2016- 18 December 2016)
Directly after the Inauguration of the Bhakti Marga Ashram Vrindavan, there is the opportunity to go on a pilgrimage with Guruji to Tamil Nadu, South India. Tamil Nadu has been the home of countless Acharyas, Saints and Avatars. It is also one of the main places of Shiva worship and various Maha Shakti Peethas. We will visit the mountain Arunachala, Maha Shakti Peethas, Sri Rangam – the main temple of all Vaishnavas and especially of the followers of Sri Sampradaya - and many more beautiful places and Saints.
Discover more at: www.bhaktimarga.org/southindia2016.
