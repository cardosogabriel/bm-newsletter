From our newest Bhakti Marga Swami:
The Grace of Our Satguru
Swami VishwaRangaRamanuja, India
Jai Gurudev! My humble prostrations to the Lotus Feet of our Satgurudev, Paramahamsa Sri Swami Vishwananda!
I met Guruji in Chidambaran, 2016. That day, I was waiting for Him and as He jumps out of the car and gets closer to me, His face starts to disappear. I saw this huge ball of light coming towards me and wasn’t sure if I was imagining or seeing something that I couldn’t understand. He held my hand as I said 'Jai Gurudev'. The moment He touched me, my entire body was vibrating, shaking. I started to cry, He grabbed me, picked me up in His arms and I said to Him as I hugged Him, 'What took you so long?'. He replied to me 'Well, I am here now'. In that moment I didn’t realize what that meant, but this was actually my Satguru telling me that He had come to pick me up and take me with Him. This was very magically because only a few years later I realised what that meant: 'I am here now'.
I think it is important for every devotee to know that Guruji is always with us in every moment. We may not understand what is going on in the moment, but the most important thing is to always remain focused on Him and to allow Him to show us what we have to be shown in this life.
There have been many incidents where Guruji had shown me what I should do and where I should be, but there was always my mind trying to sabotage this beautiful journey with my Satguru.
I cannot express the magnitude of patience He has with us. His aim is that we some day accept our dharma, the service to the Guru and to humanity. He knows best about everything; He knows best when to do what. After fully trusting Him and accepting His words from the core of my heart, my life has changed so much. It all just starts with acceptance, with being aware and with allowing Him to take you to the place where He wants you to be.
Today I know that Guruji is not just a living Master, but He is God incarnate in this world. Every single day my first prayer for everyone is: 'Please Guruji help us all to realise You more and more! Please Guruji help us all to see You in everyone! Please Guruji help us to serve each other as Your devotees! Please Guruji protect us, guide us and make us feel love, make us feel care, make us feel Your Grace all the time! Please Guruji guide us in every moment, that every action, every word, every thought is focused on You and our seva to Your Lotus Feet!'
There is no greater sangha than Bhakti Marga Sangha in the 21st century. It is the greatest blessing to share this divine journey back to the Feet of Paramatma through the Grace of our Satguru!
Swami VishwaRangaRamanuja
