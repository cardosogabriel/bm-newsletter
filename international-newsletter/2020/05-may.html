MAY 2020, #61
Isolation and connection
Swami Vishwakarunanandama, SPN
Living during these times of great change and upheaval, on every level, can be seen as an amazing blessing from Guruji. Bringing us a deeper experience of Love to His devotees and Mother Earth.
At Bhakti Marga, there are two prominent experiences at this time: isolation and connection. At first glance, they seem two different ends of a spectrum, but that masks a deep relation between them on our spiritual path.
Our experience at times can feel increasingly solitary, leading to the ultimate goal of experiencing God everywhere. By realising God within our being, we become connected to every human being and all His earthly creation.
During this time, in individual/family isolation, Guruji has emphasised what a great blessing it is for us to have “time” to go inwards. To experience a deeper and deeper peace arising from the limitation on our external activities.
Time has been created for us to focus on sadhana, scripture and daily ceremonies. Time has been given to us to purify and become aware of our samskaras. Time has been found for us to reflect and experience our connection to our atma. Time has been created for us to deepen our relationships with family and friends.
Time has been offered for us to reflect why we are here and what is our purpose. Time has been given for us to reflect and deepen our relationship with Guruji.
Our isolation, by limiting our external activities, is bringing about a deep healing for Mother Earth. Equally, our daily prayers with Guruji giving our Love to Mother Earth activates a deeper healing for Mother Earth and all Her creation.
The external material technology, like the internet with Zoom videoconferences, enables us to connect globally with each other while supporting our inner work in isolation. Every day when I listen Guruji's satsangs, I feel I am sitting at His feet. Soon we will be able to take darshan online. The isolation is encouraging us to connect globally, via technology, with each other and with spiritual searchers in different ways, whether it is OM Chanting, meditation, scripture or ceremonies.
With this technology, our outer connection helps us develop a deeper awareness of our atma while in isolation. Ultimately connecting us with every atma worldwide without the technology.
Our awareness of God's Love is uplifted - as our isolation and connection come together. The more our consciousness rises amongst Bhakti Marga devotees and Guruji's love is shared with other people, the greater the benefit for the world.
Guruji's daily prayer for Mother Earth is our daily inspiration and is spreading God's Love worldwide.
sarvasya cāhaḿ hṛdi sanniviṣṭo
'I am located in the hearts of all.'
Bhagavad Gita, Chapter 15, verse 15

Message of the Month
Jai Gurudev, everybody! Welcome back!
Today, [during the online Live Japa session] there were some messages where people were asking what I am doing in the bag. Actually, we are chanting the Divine Name, so inside the bag there is a rosary called mala and we rotate it like this inside, mala beads, so we are chanting on each bead, and that is 108 Names of God.
Today was interesting. I was looking outside earlier and I noticed that there are lots of planes flying around, also helicopters, so I was wondering what is happening, maybe it’s back again to normality. So… I don’t know. Now it’s quiet again.
