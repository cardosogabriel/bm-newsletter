APRIL 2020, #60
Stay Positive
Jai Gurudev, Everyone:
As we look back at this week, we are faced with a world that has shifted dramatically within just a matter of days.
Bhakti Marga, the global mission of our Guru Paramahamsa Vishwananda, has for 15 years provided a deep spiritual perspective on what sustains our world. The core of our mission is JUST LOVE. It highlights the essence of life, points to who we are and why we are here on this most extraordinary planet: to realise that God is Love, that we are a part of Him, and that at the core of ourselves, as soul-beings, we are also this Love. Behind everything we can see with our senses, we perceive a higher truth - a divine plan is at work.
This perception is particularly important in a time in which fear spreads fast. Fear of disease, fear of suffering, material loss, isolation and confinement, and the ultimate fear, the fear of dying. In our pursuit of happiness, our lower human nature makes us disconnect from the higher Truth. We end up lost and confused and can no longer recognise the purpose of what our life on this planet is all about: loving and realising God, looking after each other, the animal kingdom and Mother Earth.
When we violate the laws of the universe, life has its own ways to teach us about Love, how to return home to our true nature, and to remind us of what is truly important. The latest warning comes in the form of this Coronavirus, which has erupted into an unprecedented crisis. To understand the full cause of the outbreak is quite complex, but what is clear now is that a change is already happening. Life as we know it has been interrupted.
We can lament about this fact, or embrace the coming weeks as what they are in truth: a unique opportunity for transformation, both as individuals, and as humanity. Imagine what you can do during the upcoming weeks! You can finally nurture your soul. You can spend quality time with the loved ones around you, whether physically or online. You can read and study uplifting content or watch inspiring videos. You can prepare delicious vegan or vegetarian meals for you and your family. You can meditate, pray and do yoga. You can complete projects that you haven’t had a chance to work on in a long time. You can deep clean your room, your home or your cellar. All the things that you normally postpone or find excuses for. In short, you can follow your heart’s desire and listen, for once, to yourself.
Imagine how much positivity can emerge from this global shift we are witnessing! After the whole crisis is over, and we look again at the numbers, it will be worth noting how many people were saved, short-term, from fewer car accidents, violent crimes, etc., due to a general slowing down of public interaction, and also long-term: how many people improved their health with a much-needed change in diet. Plus Mother Earth will get to take a much-needed break from us polluting and mistreating her, too.
So...slowing down, reflecting, and considering a change in our self-centred lifestyle is a good thing. Right now, under these extraordinary circumstances, a positive transformation has a real chance. And it starts with each one of us. We at Bhakti Marga strongly believe that humanity as a whole will come out of this stronger than before: more humble, more grateful, and more loving.
We invite you to stay tuned to our Facebook page and, no matter where you are, join Paramahamsa Vishwananda and Bhakti Marga in activities online, and you are welcome to virtually connect with your country's Bhakti Marga family, too.
With Love,
The Swamis and Swaminis of Paramahamsa Vishwananda and Bhakti Marga
