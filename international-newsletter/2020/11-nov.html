NOV 2020, #67
FAR AWAY, YET AS NEAR AS THE HEART
Swami Kanjalochanananda, Croatia
Jai Gurudev,
In these challenging times, when the whole world is trembling with fear and uncertainty, being a devotee of Paramahamsa Vishwananda gives shelter and security to all who humbly approach Him with love and devotion. What can we learn from this situation?
Whatever life lays before us, we must accept as God’s grace, whether we like it or not. Being physically away from Guruji is not easy, and we all may have taken for granted coming and seeing Guruji whenever we desired to do so. But with all the restrictions now, it is not possible, or it is very difficult, for most of us. Yet, it is a great opportunity for all of us that, instead of running outside and looking for fulfilment and love everywhere else, we turn inwardly and find Him in the deep core of our hearts.
Still, despite all this, I deeply desired so much to be with Guruji and with the Divine Mother during the Navaratri festival. Usually, I don’t pray for ‘petty’ things, but this meant a lot to me. When Guruji gave an Online Darshan, my intense prayer was to be able to travel and see Guruji for Navaratri. His response was instantaneous as He spoke with me right after He gave darshan to all participants. After a few words of exchange, He concluded that there is always a way and Giridhari will clear it. And that I understood as a blessing and an invitation.
As He had blessed me, the way had been cleared for me, and without difficulty I was able to reach my destination: the home of the Divine Mother and Guruji. I was not the only one who received this blessing, though. All the devotees who were gathered for the darshan in the Serbian Bhakti Marga temple experienced an instantaneous transformation. Their faith in Guruji also strengthened, as they all understood how Guruji can remove all obstacles for His devotees, no matter where the devotees may physically be.
Swami Kanjalochana
