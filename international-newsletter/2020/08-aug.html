AUG 2020, #64
From our newest Bhakti Marga Swami:
Virtual Ashram
Swamini Vsihwaprabhaavati Mata, Spain
Jai Gurudev everybody!
Last February, I was in Shree Peetha Nilaya for the Country Week, and Guruji asked me about my travel to South America. Then He said: 'Swamini, this time you will stay a little bit longer...' Of course, at that time I didn't know exactly what He meant, but soon after I landed in Argentina, it was clear. And it's so nice to see how Guruji arranged everything perfectly. Last time He was here last year, I was telling Him how important the Country Week had been for the people here, as it really helped them to understand everything better and do more seva.
These months here have been a wonderful gift from Him to everybody. Some days ago, one new devotee was telling me that we probably wouldn't have been able to do what we have done even in more than a year. Guruji being with us every day during the quarantine has been a great opportunity for them to do a "crash course" on His teachings, and as everybody was at home, even without thinking about it, Guruji gave us the chance to have our "virtual ashram".
Prayers, bhajans, meditations, BM Sadhana practices, puja, BM Academy courses, satsang with many different devotees... After some weeks I was amazed to see that we really had like a long Country Week with almost all the Spanish-speaking countries. What Guruji has always stressed is that it’s very important (no matter if it is in AKY, OMC, puja, or whatever other practice in BM) to build community, and it was a real fact that it was taking place.
I cannot express the magnitude of patience He has with us. His aim is that we some day accept our dharma, the service to the Guru and to humanity. He knows best about everything; He knows best when to do what. After fully trusting Him and accepting His words from the core of my heart, my life has changed so much. It all just starts with acceptance, with being aware and with allowing Him to take you to the place where He wants you to be.
Moreover, He gave us the cherry on the cake: we could take part in the Sangha Facilitator Training, which, for the first time, was done online, from the end of May til mid-July. I couldn't believe that these Spanish-speaking countries so far away, had the chance to meet the rest of the devotees, and what to me was more important, that they could live and experience in the training what we really do and what we really are. And to do this with participants of 20 different countries, giving them another different insight, and also another feeling of family.
It's somehow funny that in this time of Corona which has been so dark for some people, it has been so bright for us! And for me personally, it has been such a wonderful and joyful time here. It makes me think of chapter 2, verse 69 of the Bhagavad Gita:
yā niśā sarva bhūtānāṁ tasyāṁ jāgarti saṁyamī | yasyāṁ jāgrati bhūtāni sā niśā paśyato muneḥ ||
What is night for all beings, is actually a time of awakening for the self-controlled, and when all beings are awake, it is night for the enlightened one.
I have simply no words to describe how Guruji cares and serves us all the time. May all devotees have trust in Him to share with others the amrit that He gives us all the time.
Much love,
Swamini Vsihwaprabhaavati Mata

Message of the Month
Jai Gurudev, everybody!
Firstly, a blessed and happy Gurupurnima to everybody!
In life, like it is said, life itself is one of the privileges that one has. It’s one of the most difficult things: to acquire a human life. In the shastra, it's said that, after passing through countless of births and deaths, you come to the point of attaining this human life. In spite of it being so difficult to attain, yet through the grace of God one receives this human life. For what?
Very often, that is a question, you know. When you look at the world, we see that people don’t realise their aim of life. Despite that many masters have come, and Bhagavan Himself has come to remind humanity, yet, even knowing how precious this human life is, people don’t do anything.
