SEP 2020, #65
YOUTH SUMMER SANGHA
Swami Keshava, SPN
Jai Gurudev!
We have recently had our yearly Youth Summer Sangha here at The Ashram. This year due to COVID restrictions we held two sangha events with a total of 68 participants. I was very happy that we were able to go ahead with the Sangha even with the current situation. For me it is an absolute highlight every year.
Many, many activities were done throughout the week. It was great teamwork trying our best to combine learning with different fun activities like quizzes and much more.
It is truly inspiring to see youngsters getting a first experience and growing on the spiritual path. Whether completely new or already a devotee for years - the dynamic of coming together with same-aged and like-minded people who are open, excited and joyful to learn about our path, to live it, experience it and share it with others is really beautiful.
The sweet simple moments of all sitting around the evening bonfires and either just singing your heart out and dancing, sharing your experiences with each other or meditating and chanting together are simply magical.
Giving each other a feeling of belonging and purpose is one of the most beautiful feelings which everyone takes home with them - the feeling of being part of this family, having found new and fostered old relationships with people who look for and love God the way you do.
It was truly beautiful to see how much time Guruji chose to spend with the group and with each one individually. He went for multiple walks with the group, taking the time to speak with each one individually.
Guruji gave a really sweet impromptu satsang in the forest highlighting the importance for each one especially in these times to be strong with oneself, to learn about the path, and most of all, encouraging us to be courageous enough to love and be happy and joyful no matter what happens in the outside world.
This time of Youth Sangha is very special for me because it’s so beautiful to see young people coming on the spiritual path, seeing them change, grow and freely expressing themselves. It is inspiring to see how excited each one is to learn about the scriptures and Guruji and walk this path together. It makes me appreciate each time, again and again, how grateful I can be to Guruji that I have had the grace to know Him from a very young age and seeing the impact He has had on my life - and giving young people the chance to have the same opportunity is the greatest joy to see.
Much love,
Swami Keshava

Message of the Month
It's lovely to see all of you.
The devotees reflect the light of the Divine. When I look at all of you, I see that radiance through your faces, and really, that brings lots of joy and happiness. That's what is missing right now in this world.
People are hanging so much onto fear that they forget to live. More than Corona is killing them, I think it's the fear that is killing them. Don't hold upon this fear. Hold upon life, and be happy in each moment. Life is a grace from Sriman Narayana, from God, and you have to honour what He has given you. And how will you honour it? By transforming yourself: by transforming your mind from a mind which is centred upon limitation to a mind which is divine, which is without any limit. That is the journey.
